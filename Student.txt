What is the school timing?
School starts at 8:30 AM and ends at 3:00 PM.

How many subjects are there?
There are 6 main subjects in each class.

Is attendance mandatory?
Yes, minimum 75% attendance is required.

Are there extracurricular activities?
Yes, sports and clubs are available.

When are exams conducted?
Exams are conducted twice a year.

Is there a uniform?
Yes, students must wear school uniform.

Are mobile phones allowed?
No, mobile phones are not allowed.

How can parents contact teachers?
Through parent-teacher meetings.

Is transport facility available?
Yes, buses are provided.

Are scholarships available?
Yes, based on merit and need.
